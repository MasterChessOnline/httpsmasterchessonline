# 📍♟️ MasterChess.live — Google Business Profile (Full Setup)

> Goal: get **MasterChess** verified on Google Business Profile (GBP) so it appears in Google Search + Maps and gains trust signals for SEO. You are **not** registering a physical location — this is an **online business**.

---

## 1. What you're actually building

A GBP listing that will:
- Show up in Google Search ("MasterChess")
- (Sometimes) show on Google Maps
- Add trust signals to the domain → better SEO

⚠️ Not a physical location. **Online business only.**

---

## 2. Step-by-step setup

### 🟢 Step 1 — Open the page
Go to: <https://www.google.com/business/> → click **Manage now**.

### 🟢 Step 2 — Business name
Enter: **MasterChess**

❌ Do **NOT** enter:
- `MasterChess.live`
- `MasterChess online chess`

Google often rejects these or down-ranks the listing.

### 🟢 Step 3 — Category (critical)
Pick: **Software company**

Alternatives (only if the above isn't accepted):
- Internet service provider
- Video game developer

👉 **Best choice: Software company.**

### 🟢 Step 4 — Location
Click: **"I don't have a physical location"**.

### 🟢 Step 5 — Service area
Add:
- Worldwide
- Europe
- Serbia

### 🟢 Step 6 — Website
Enter: `https://masterchess.live`

### 🟢 Step 7 — Contact
- Email: `support@masterchess.live` (or any address you own)
- Phone: leave blank (not required)

---

## 3. Business description (copy–paste)

```
MasterChess is an online chess platform where players can compete in ranked matches, participate in tournaments, and solve daily chess puzzles. The platform focuses on competitive gameplay, ranking progression, and community-driven chess experiences.
```

---

## 4. Photos you MUST upload

Google wants proof the business exists. Upload **at least 3–5**:
1. Homepage screenshot (`masterchess.live`)
2. Chess board / gameplay screenshot
3. Tournament page screenshot
4. Discord server screenshot
5. MasterChess logo

---

## 5. Verification (the most important part)

### 📹 Video verification (most common for online businesses)
Record a single continuous video showing:

1. **The site** — open `https://masterchess.live`, show the homepage.
2. **Proof it's yours** — open the admin panel / dashboard or backend login.
3. **Activity** — Discord server, active tournaments, online players.

Say out loud:
> "This is MasterChess, an online chess platform with tournaments and ranked games."

### 📩 Alternative verification methods
- Email (works if you have a domain email like `support@masterchess.live`)
- Phone SMS
- Postcard (rare for online businesses)

---

## 6. Mistakes that will get you rejected

- ❌ Fake address
- ❌ "Fake office" location
- ❌ Spammy categories ("free chess", "best chess site", etc.)
- ❌ Changing the business name right after verification

---

## 7. How to pass verification easily

Before submitting for verification, make sure:
- The site is live (no "coming soon")
- These pages exist and load:
  - **Play** page (`/play`)
  - **About** page (`/about`)
  - **Tournaments** page (`/tournaments`)
- Discord invite link is visible on the site
- Domain email is set up (helps a lot)

👉 Google literally checks: *"Does this thing actually exist?"*
