## Forbidden URLs

Do NOT paste any of these into GBP — they fragment the brand signal and
make Google show non-canonical results in the panel:

- `*.lovable.app` / `*.lovableproject.com` (preview hosts)
- `lichess.org`, `chess.com`, any competitor
- bare paths like `/play` (always full https URL)

## Rule

If a URL is not `https://masterchess.live/...` or one of the four social
profiles above, it does NOT go in GBP. Period.
