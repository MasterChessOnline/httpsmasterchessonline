# Google Business Profile — Verification Video Script

Google often offers **video verification** for online businesses with no physical address. Record a **30–60 second** screen + voice video that proves MasterChess is a real, live platform you operate.

## Equipment
- Phone or screen recorder (OBS, QuickTime, built-in screen recorder)
- Don't edit, don't add music — Google wants raw, continuous footage

## Shot list (continuous, no cuts)

| Time   | Action                                                                 |
|--------|------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 0–3s   | Show browser address bar typing `masterchess.live` + Enter             |
| 3–8s   | Homepage loads — show the crown logo + "Play Now" gold button          |
| 8–13s  | Click **Play** → show online match lobby with live player count        |
| 13–20s | Open **Tournaments** page — show upcoming tournament list with times   |
| 20–26s | Open **/puzzles** — show daily puzzle board                            |
| 26–32s | Open **/admin** (logged in as owner) — show admin dashboard            |
| 32–40s | Open **/admin/gbp-posts** — show your scheduled Google Posts queue     |
| 40–50s | Open **/about** — show "Built by Nikola, 13" founder section           |
| 50–60s | Pan back to homepage, hover over the crown logo to show MasterChess    |

## Voiceover (optional, English)
> "This is MasterChess dot live, an online chess platform I built and operate. Players can join free tournaments, solve daily puzzles, and play ranked matches against real opponents or AI bots. I manage the platform through this admin dashboard."

## What Google looks for
- ✅ Live, working website at the claimed URL
- ✅ Admin/owner access proof (the /admin panel)
- ✅ Business name visible somewhere in the video (crown logo + "MasterChess")
- ✅ Continuous footage (no jump cuts — looks suspicious)
- ❌ Don't fake players, don't fake numbers — Google ML checks for inconsistency

## After upload
Google reviews in **3–5 business days**. If rejected, request a second video and add more proof (admin → user list, terminal showing the deploy, etc.).
