# Growth Playbook — from 0 to 100 real users

Concrete acquisition + retention ideas for MasterChess. Sorted by impact/effort.
The DB Chess Cup on **18 July 2026, 16:00 CEST** is the current hero campaign;
every idea below should route users toward that event or toward Quick Match.

## Tier 1 — Ship first (highest ROI)

1. **Referral loop** — each user gets `/r/{code}`. Inviter + invitee both get
   250 coins + a "Founder Friend" badge. Live counter on the profile.
2. **One-tap post-game share card** — after every win, generate a PNG
   (final position + rating gain + username + masterchess.live) and share to
   WhatsApp / Instagram / X with one tap.
3. **DB Cup countdown FOMO** — hero banner + sticky ribbon show live
   registered/max count and a real-time countdown to 18 Jul 16:00 CEST.
4. **Signup carrot** — 500 coins + "Founder 100" badge for the first 100
   verified accounts. Show a live "X of 100 spots left" counter on `/signup`.
5. **Guest → account nudge** — after 1 guest game, soft-prompt
   "Save your rating in 1 tap" with Google OAuth.

## Tier 2 — Next pass

6. Daily login streak — surface the reward chain on the home.
7. `/vs/{code}` challenge-a-friend link, WhatsApp-first CTA.
8. Weekly leaderboard with a public URL — top 3 pinned to home = social proof.
9. Public DB Cup "wall of names" (indexed) — SEO magnet and ego share.
10. Discord + X webhook on: new tournament registration, top-10 finish,
    big upsets.

## Tier 3 — Content / SEO (compounds over weeks)

11. Country landing pages (`/chess/serbia`, `/chess/germany`) with local
    player count.
12. "Beat the bot" landing per bot (`/beat/{botId}`) with OG share card.
13. Daily puzzle auto-post to X + Discord with FEN preview image.
14. Long-tail blog: "best free chess site 2026", "chess vs bots free",
    "how to play chess online with friends".
15. Coordinate a "Play me live on MasterChess" video with DailyChess_12.

## Tier 4 — Community flywheel

16. Prominent "Create your clan" CTA for new users on Home.
17. Discord auto-role by rating tier (bragging rights → retention).
18. Opt-in push: "friend online", "tournament starts in 15 min".

## Marketing distribution — where to post

- **Reddit**: /r/chess, /r/chessbeginners (weekly self-promo, no spam)
- **Instagram Reels + TikTok**: 15s "beat the bot" fails, DB Cup countdown
- **YouTube shorts** via DailyChess_12
- **Facebook groups**: Serbian chess clubs, hobby groups
- **WhatsApp**: direct referrals — highest conversion in SRB/EU region
- **X/Twitter**: daily puzzle bot + tournament highlights
- **Google Business Profile** posts weekly (calendar already in /docs)
